Citing Hyperspy¶

If Hyperspy has been significant to a project that leads to an academic publication, please acknowledge that fact by citing one or more of the publications below and, if possible, give a link to the Hyperspy website. At present, we are still in the process of writing an academic reference about Hyperspy. However, under its previous name, EELSLab, it has been used by its authors and/or collaborators in a number of scientific articles that you can cite ( see Peer-review articles with results obtained using Hyperspy)

  • If you have used Hyperspy for curve fitting please cite Arenal et al. 2008 or de la Peña et al. 2010 `get the pdf here <https://sites.google.com/site/lfzagonel/manuscripts/2010SurfaceScienceI.pdf`_
  • If you have used Hyperspy for PCA and or ICA of images please cite Sarahan et al. 2011
  • If you have used Hyperspy for PCA and or ICA of spectra please cite de la Peña et al. 2011

Thank you!
